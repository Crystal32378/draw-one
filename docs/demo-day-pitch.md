Pick a God.

Draw one.

## Project Context

Draw One was created by non-technical professionals as a culturally grounded decision-support ritual interface. The demo translated a familiar Taiwanese ritual behavior into a simple digital product flow:

1. Pick a God.
2. Write your question.
3. Draw one.
